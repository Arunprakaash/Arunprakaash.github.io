Game Designer

Role: Android Game Developer Intern
Duration: December 2020 - June 2021
Client: CoCognito (www.cocognito.com)
Project: MathCraze (Available on Android Play Store)

During my tenure as an intern, I contributed to the development of “MathCraze,” a game that challenges and entertains users with math-based puzzles. My role involved extensive work in game development within the Unity engine, integrating Firebase for backend functionality, and deploying the game to the Android Play Store. My commitment to learning and improving the gaming experience was recognized by the team, and I was commended for my ability to transform requirements into efficient, working code.
